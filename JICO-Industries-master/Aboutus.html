About Us

Jico Solar was established in 1994 and has 24 years of experience with the passion to build a business whose products and services make an optimistic and long-term contribution to the world. Jico Solar is one of the most innovative solar product manufacturers and distributors. Jico Solar dispenses its high performing, reliable and affordable energy solutions and services to the commercial and residential customers. Solar technology is proven and reliable to make the transition to clean, renewable energy. Among all the benefits of solar panels, the most important thing is that solar energy is a truly renewable energy source. It can be harnessed in all areas of the world and is available every day. Jico Solar is dedicated to enabling the widespread adoption of solar energy and believe, it is imperative for the future health of mankind and our planet. Jico Solar is devoted to emerging solutions to overcome barriers to the adoption of solar energy and help to make it a mainstream source of power. High load use this battery power, it is capable of delivering more energy in a short time.

Jico Solar is the brand name for the products marketed by Jico Solar Industries an ISO 9001: 2015 accredited company. The ISO 9001:2015 standard has helped Jico solar develop a Quality Management System to meet our customers’ quality requirements while continually improving operational processes. The result of using ISO 9001 standards has been shorter lead times, high delivery reliability, and consistently high quality of services and products, which benefits both Jico solar customers and our business.

Mission

Delivering the cleanest, most efficient and economic solar energy solution and contributing to the global development with our top expertise in renewable energy, leveraging our ability to work at the crossroads of business and technology.

Vision

To provide comprehensive solution to clean energy and advance this industry by serving our clients and creating measurable value for them. Contribute to innovation and growth in renewable energy across the globe.



Jico transcends the passion to empower customers and simply create an amicable environment with solar energy solutions. Technology in the solar power industry is constantly evolving and developments would strengthen the future with a clean environment.
