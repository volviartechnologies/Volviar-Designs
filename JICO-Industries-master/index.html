About Us

Jico Solar was established in 1994 and has 24 years of experience with the passion to build a business whose products and services make an optimistic and long-term contribution to the world. Jico Solar is one of the most innovative solar product manufacturers and distributors.
Jico Solar is the brand name for the products marketed by Jico Solar Industries an ISO 9001: 2015 accredited company. The result of using ISO 9001 standards has been shorter lead times, high delivery reliability, and consistently high quality of services and products, which benefits both Jico solar customers and our business.

Earn from Electricity Sales

Clients who have installed solar rooftop photovoltaic (PV) systems can now sell electricity to power distribution companies. A process called net-metering allows the clients to sell any excess power generated. When electrical needs of the clients are met, the additional electricity is sent out to the grid through the utility meter.
